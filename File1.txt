The university has five campuses based in different cities and was 
the first multi-campus university in Pakistan.These campuses are
located in Chiniot-Faisalabad, Islamabad, Karachi, Lahore and Peshawar. 
It was inaugurated by President Pervez Musharraf in 2000. It is consistently 
ranked among the leading institutions of higher education in the country 
and ranked top in computer sciences and information technology by the 
Higher Education Commission of Pakistan in 2010.
Its engineering programs have been accredited with Pakistan Engineering Council.
In-collaboration with the Open Source Applications Foundation (OSAF),
it also provides interest-free loans as financial aid to its students.
